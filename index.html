About Plants vs Zombies

Plants vs Zombies is a popular tower defense game where players defend their homes from zombies by strategically placing various plants with unique abilities. Each plant serves a different purpose in your defense strategy, from shooting peas to producing sun energy.

Originally developed by PopCap Games, Plants vs Zombies has become a beloved franchise known for its charming graphics, humorous characters, and addictive gameplay. The game balances strategy with accessibility, making it enjoyable for players of all ages.

How to Play

1. Collect Sun
Click on falling suns to collect solar energy, which is used as currency to deploy plants.
2. Place Plants
Select a plant from your inventory, then click on a lawn tile to place it. Different plants have different costs and abilities.
3. Defend Your Lawn
Stop zombies from reaching your house by creating an effective defense with your plants.
4. Win Levels
Defeat all zombie waves to complete a level and progress through the game, unlocking new plants and facing tougher zombies.
